About Dementia

Prognosis and Diagnosis of Early Dementia is an irregular heartbeat that disrupts normal heart function, potentially causing symptoms like palpitations and dizziness. Diagnosis relies on tests like electrocardiograms, and treatments range from medication and lifestyle changes to medical devices or surgery, depending on severity and underlying causes.

We have used the data set available in Kaggle - Prognosis and Diagnosis of Early Dementia. After Classifying the data, Preprocessin and performed the Exploratory Data Analysis. This data set contains about 1356 records of data in various categories.

How it will works

The project entails creating an IoT and web-based system for real-time Prognosis and Diagnosis of Early Dementia detection. It involves developing compact, wireless IoT devices with sensors to monitor cardiac parameters like heart rate and ECG. Algorithms and machine learning models are employed for real-time analysis, accurately identifying arrhythmias. A web-based dashboard provides healthcare professionals and patients with visualizations of vital signs, alerts for detected arrhythmias, and historical data. The system is designed to be scalable, reliable, and user-friendly, with integration capabilities for existing healthcare systems. Testing, validation, documentation, and training are crucial steps for successful deployment in clinical settings.
